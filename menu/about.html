About Our Society

Accessible Vision Society is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering visually impaired individuals. We focus on providing high-quality education and equal opportunities, helping our students grow and succeed in every part of life.

Our Mission

To uplift visually impaired individuals by teaching them practical skills. We want to ensure everyone can lead an independent and dignified life through education and support.

Our Vision

We believe that education is a fundamental right for everyone. Our goal is to build an inclusive and accessible society where physical limitations never stop someone from learning.

Our Story

Established in 2021, Accessible Vision Society has spent the last few years building a supportive learning community. We started with a simple goal: to make education affordable and accessible for the visually impaired. Today, we continue to expand our reach, using digital tools to bring classes to students wherever they are.

Our Core Programs

Academic Excellence

We provide specialized coaching for competitive and government examinations to help students achieve their career goals.

Skill Development

Our training focuses on digital literacy, helping students use computers and mobile devices with ease and confidence.

Inclusive Learning

We use accessible teaching methods to ensure that all our online classes are convenient and completely barrier-free.
