Digital Assistance Centre (Cyber Café)

Empowering Lives Through Technology, Making Every Opportunity Accessible.

Many important opportunities today, such as admissions, competitive examinations, scholarships, jobs, and government services, require online applications. However, inaccessible websites and digital barriers often make these services difficult for persons with visual impairment to use independently.

To support digital inclusion, Accessible Vision Society has established its Digital Assistance Centre (Cyber Café). We provide reliable, affordable, and accessible assistance with online form filling so that every individual can apply with confidence and dignity.

Our Services

  • Competitive examination application forms
  • School, college, and university admission forms
  • Scholarship and fellowship applications
  • Google Forms and online registrations
  • Government and private organization application forms

Please Note: We provide assistance only with online form filling. Eligibility, admissions, examination results, recruitment, and selection decisions are made by the respective organizations.

Service Charges:
• Google Forms / General Online Forms – ₹30 per form
• Competitive examination and admission forms – Affordable charges based on the complexity of the application.
